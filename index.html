Daniel J. Hoffman

Software Engineer · Researcher

Background

I'm a software engineer who came up through health informatics research. I spent several years at MedStar Health's National Center for Human Factors in Healthcare studying how clinicians interact with EHR systems, building data pipelines, and publishing work on patient safety and clinical workflow. That work led to a U.S. patent and around 15 journal publications.

Current Work

These days I build production systems — cloud infrastructure on AWS, full-stack applications in TypeScript, and CI/CD pipelines that actually work. I like solving real problems with clean, maintainable code and figuring out how things fit together end to end.

Research

Published work on EHR usability, patient safety, and clinical workflow, appearing in Applied Clinical Informatics, Journal of Patient Safety, JAMIA, and others.
